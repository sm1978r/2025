About HelloHelp

HelloHelp is a specialized support Mobile App designed for registered customers who require assistance with their internet setup, set top box installation, or TV equipment and connectivity set up. Our platform enables existing registered users to easily connect with our customer support team via video, voice, or chat.

How It Works

Step 1

Download the HelloHelp app from the App Store or Google Play.

Step 2

Enter your mobile number and confirm your identity using a one-time password (OTP).

Step 3

Choose a support option: Video, Chat, or Call.

Step 4

Connect instantly with our trained support experts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to use HelloHelp?

HelloHelp app is available for download but support is available only for registered users. If your phone number is not on file, you will be asked to provide your account number to verify your eligibility.

What types of support does HelloHelp offer?

HelloHelp provides assistance with internet setup, set top box installation, TV equipment and connectivity set up, TV equipment and connectivity set up, billing queries, and technical support for registered users*.

Can I use HelloHelp without registration?

No. You must be a verified and active customer. Support is not available for unregistered or expired accounts.

How do I verify my registration?

Download the app and contact HelloHelp to confirm the status of your registration.
